Our Story

Crafted with Passion,
Worn with Pride

Awal Stitches is a premium Nigerian fashion house dedicated to elevating traditional and contemporary style. From richly embroidered native wears to sharp formal suits, every piece we create is a testament to the artistry of our craftsmen and the elegance of our clients.

We believe clothing is more than fabric — it is identity, confidence, and culture. Whether you seek a bespoke native agbada for a ceremony or a tailored senator wear for business, we bring your vision to life with precision and care.
